Dracaena trifasciata hort

CLASSIFICATION

ORDER : Asparagales
FAMILY : Asparagaceae
GENUS : Dracaena
SPECIES : trifasciata
BOTANICAL NAME : Dracaena trifasciata hort. ex Prain
SYNONYMS : ---
COMMON NAME : Snake plant
TAMIL NAME : Marul
HINDI NAME : ---

PLANT DESCRIPTION

HABIT :  A perennial herb with short thick rhizome.
LEAVES :  Erect with leaves long, leathery, linear-lanceolate, deep green with light green to grey-white waxy cross bands.
INFLORESCENCE :  Raceme
FLOWERS :  Greenish white in colour.

MEDICINAL USES

The plant is used to treat ringworm and fungal diseases.
The leaf sap is applied directly on infected sores and cuts.
Purifies air as they have the ability to remove toxic air pollutants.
Possess anti diabetic properties.
